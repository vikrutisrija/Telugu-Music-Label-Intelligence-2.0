## What is this?

A free, live tool built specifically for the Telugu music ecosystem.
No login. No cost. No pitch.

It answers one question most music tools ignore:

**Not how big is your audience — but what is your audience actually worth?**

---

## What is inside

### ❤️ Audience Value Score
Six plain questions any label owner can answer in 2 minutes.
Score out of 100 based on how well you convert listeners into
communities, paying fans, and brand-worthy ecosystems.
Includes a personalised 90-day action plan.

### 🌱 Fan Ecosystem Journey
Six stages — Discovery to Advocacy.
At every stage: what most Telugu labels do now,
what they should do instead, and one immediate action.

### 💰 Revenue Calculator
Enter your subscriber count and audience engagement level.
Get an instant estimate of direct monthly revenue potential
across fan memberships, brand partnerships, and creator programs.

### 🤖 AI Strategy Chat
Trained on real case studies — Republic Records,
BTS Weverse, T-Series, Def Jam.
Ask anything about Telugu music label growth.
